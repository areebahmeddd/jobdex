# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a welcoming and respectful space for everyone. Contributors are expected to treat each other with professionalism regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Expected Behaviors

- Communicate respectfully and constructively
- Accept feedback without hostility
- Acknowledge mistakes and correct them
- Prioritize the health of the project and community over individual preferences

## Unacceptable Behaviors

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted advances
- Sharing someone's private information without their consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject any contribution or comment that violates this Code of Conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project.

## Reporting

Report violations by opening a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/areebahmeddd/jobdex/issues) or contacting a maintainer directly. All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement Guidelines

1. **Warning** - A private written warning for a first or minor violation.
2. **Temporary Restriction** - A time-limited suspension from participating in discussions or submitting contributions.
3. **Temporary Ban** - A suspension from all project spaces with conditions for reinstatement.
4. **Permanent Ban** - Permanent removal, reserved for repeated serious violations.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces including the repository, issues, pull requests, and any official communication channels.
